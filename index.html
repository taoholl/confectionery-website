Life just got a little sweeter

  • Meet Beau

    It all started 14 years ago when a young Beau found her self babysitting a friends child. The child despretly wanted candy and Beau did not have any money to buy her some. She went into the kitchen and created something sweet for the child. "It turned out awful" Beau said, but seeing that child light up she knew that she was onto something. 5 years later her curosity for candy making turned into a passion and she opened up her first shop on suger lane. Today that little shop is known as the best place to get candy in all North America!
